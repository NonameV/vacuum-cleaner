About Jet 70

The Samsung Jet 70 Pet Cordless Stick Vacuum is your go-to for everyday cleaning. Featuring 150 Air Watts* of suction power and a dust filter that traps around 99.999%** of microdust, you’ll vacuum the many surfaces of your home with incredible precision. Use your vacuum for up to 40 minutes of uninterrupted cleaning before a recharge is needed or easily swap the battery with a spare.*** The lightweight design makes it easy to vacuum stairs, reach beneath sofas, and clean high cabinets. Enjoy many different specialized brushes and tools to choose from, with the highlight being the Turbo Action Brush; rotating more than 3,700 times a minute and with 180° swivel flexibility, the brush cleans dust, hair, and other debris on multiple surfaces. Use the cordless vacuum cleaner without interruption as consistent suction prevents dirt build-up on the filter**** and rest easy knowing the filter is dishwasher safe.
